Important Information for Video Uploads

To ensure a fair and smooth judging process, please follow these guidelines for your dance video submissions:

  • **Clear Video Quality:** Record your video in a well-lit environment with clear audio. Ensure your entire body is visible throughout the performance.
  • **Video Length:** Keep your video submissions concise! Videos should not exceed 3 minutes in length.
  • **Music Editing:** **Do not** rely on the web app to play background music. Please edit your dance video to include the desired music directly within the video file itself. This ensures compatibility and avoids potential playback issues.

By following these guidelines, you'll showcase your talent in the best possible light! Good luck!
